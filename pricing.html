Frequently asked questions

Do I need to provide water?
No - we bring water and all professional equipment. All you need to do is provide access to your vehicle and a power source.
Do you come to me?
Yes - that's the whole point. We come to your home, office, or wherever your car is parked. No drop-off required, no waiting at a shop.
Do you require a deposit?
No deposit required to book. Payment is collected after the job is complete and you're happy with the results.
How long does a detail take?
An interior detail typically takes 1.5-2 hours. A full interior + exterior detail usually takes 2-4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We'll give you an accurate estimate when you book.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, cash, and Zelle. Payment is collected after the service is complete.
Why is Clean Shine more expensive than other detailers?
You're getting Jordan personally - the owner - on every single job. Not a hired tech, not a subcontractor. That means consistent results, real accountability, and someone who genuinely cares about your car because his reputation depends on it.
Do you offer maintenance plans?
Yes - monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly plans with locked-in lower rates and priority booking. No contracts, cancel anytime.
What types of vehicles do you service?
Coupes, sedans, SUVs, trucks, and vans. Pricing varies by vehicle size. Contact us for a custom quote on oversized vehicles or specialty cars.
How do I book?
Book online using the button at the top of the page, or call/text us directly at (480) 799-7248. We typically have availability within the same week.
